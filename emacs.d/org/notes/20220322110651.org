#+title: Method of steepest descent
#+filetags: :stub:

In physics this is used for the [[id:6e12566d-9909-4870-9837-a983dc605852][Semi-classical limit]]. It allows us to calculate the orders of an
expected value as as [[id:bafc5759-1098-48b7-b9ff-ec458d629254][Asymptotic series]] in \( \hbar \). The [[id:6695e737-590b-4802-9448-66d370363fc0][Stationary phase approximation]] recovers
only the classical limit, i.e. the [[id:c97faab6-ff15-4b84-8414-946ddbe13894][Principle of least action]].
