#+title: Point mass distribution
#+filetags: :stub:

The point mass distribution is a [[id:ebbac5a1-47e3-4112-bd8a-a1511f50add0][Discrete probability distribution]] where all the probability mass
is concentrated at a single point. In other words, if \( X \sim \delta_a \) then \( \mathbb{P}(X = a) = 1 \).

[[id:ef5d83c9-c716-40dc-a5d9-5c079a51c9c0][The point mass distribution is the only distribution with vanishing standard deviation]].
