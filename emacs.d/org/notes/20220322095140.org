#+title: Airy function
#+filetags: :stub:

It appears in quantum mechanics for a particle in a constant force field.

\[
\mathrm{Ai}(x) = \frac{1}{\pi} \int\limits_0^{\infty} \cos \left( \frac{t^3}{3} + xt \right) \, dt
\]
