#+title: Chi-squared distribution

The chi-squared distribution has a single parameter \( k \) known as the degrees of freedom.

The [[id:df57d740-6908-4271-809b-8d9e719cef01][chi-squared distribution is a gamma distribution]].

The [[id:a458206d-530e-49d0-aed0-e45850933bfb][chi-squared distribution is the sum of squares of independent standard normal distributions]].
