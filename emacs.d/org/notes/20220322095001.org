#+title: Bessel function
#+filetags: :stub:

Appears in problems with circular or cylindrical symmetry. Can be related to the [[id:bb3c3484-1727-4564-acb1-475283038b3e][Incomplete gamma function]].

\[
J_0(x) = \frac{1}{\pi} \int\limits_0^{\pi} \cos \left( x \sin t \right) \, dt
\]
