#+title: Extreme value theory
#+filetags: :stub:

Extreme value theory deals with the distribution of extremes in a sample such as the minimum,
maximum or any point far away from the medians.

A key result in extreme value theory is the [[id:0085d6ca-564c-4245-af54-7f203f2756a5][Fisher-Tippett-Gnedenko theorem]].
