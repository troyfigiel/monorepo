#+title: Interpretable models should be used for high-stake decisions
#+filetags: :stub:

For a high-stake decision, it is important that we know why a model comes to a certain decision. It
is important to use [[id:64287899-3f32-4593-a327-b06fdf7d9f1d][Interpretable machine learning]] in such cases.
