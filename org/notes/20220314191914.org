#+title: Zipfian distribution
#+filetags: :stub:

\[
f_{N, s}(k) = \frac{k^{-s}}{H_{N,s}}
\]

where \( H_{N,s} \) is the N-th [[id:2f369ec4-8f30-473c-b527-c529954069e6][Generalized harmonic number]].

Since \( \lim_{N \rightarrow \infty} H_{N, s} = \zeta(s) \), the limit of the Zipfian distribution
is the [[id:2ca0b709-60e1-4e5f-a9fd-7ec25da6e088][Zeta distribution]].
