#+title: Normal distribution
#+filetags: :stub:

Also known as a Gaussian distribution or more informally as a bell curve, is a probability distribution defined over \( \mathbb{R} \) by the probability density

\[ f(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt(2 \pi) \sigma} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{x - \mu}{\sigma} \right)^2} \]

When \( \mu = 0 \) and \( \sigma = 1 \), we call the distribution the [[id:5bcb8ba4-6c5e-460b-abc6-d9990b2b8061][standard normal distribution]].
