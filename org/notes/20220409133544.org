#+title: Quadratic form
#+filetags: :stub:

There is a one-to-one correspondence between quadratic forms and [[id:03e4fed5-fb2c-40a2-b93a-627b202bc906][Symmetric matrices]]. In particular,
this means we can always diagonalize a quadratic form.
