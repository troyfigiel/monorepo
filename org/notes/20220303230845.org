#+title: Factorial moment
#+filetags: :stub:

The factorial moments are expectation values of [[id:27eb39d1-309a-4d6b-a782-963ddc0fdbd3][Falling factorials]]. They are useful for studying
the moments of non-negative integer valued random variables.

[[id:9616ff81-f023-496b-8f36-0aa7fbc547fb][Factorial moments are related to raw moments through Stirling numbers of the second kind]].

They can be calculated through the [[id:e91baf7b-0062-4750-ac07-d4ecea189938][Factorial moment generating function]].
