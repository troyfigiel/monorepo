#+title: Confusion matrix
#+filetags: :stub:

The confusion matrix is a 2x2-dimensional table that visualizes the performance of a [[id:ad3e4599-2e42-4f62-a937-85f26386d57f][Classification]]
algorithm, most commonly a [[id:a798bb8f-fda8-439e-9977-783be6b9b37e][Binary classification]] algorithm. It is an example of a [[id:961b0d54-650d-4517-bc88-b4cc63a3b771][Contingency table]].
