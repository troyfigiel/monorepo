Altair embeds all data in the front-end so it is not well-suited for larger datasets.

Not only comes with [[id:74875146-ef52-4fe1-bcbc-859f039639c7][Grammar of graphics]], but also has its own idea of [[id:2cce02b0-6f86-4b58-adeb-12758bcd6e2d][Grammar of interactions]].
