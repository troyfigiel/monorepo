#+title: Note taking as a tool for learning
#+filetags: :stub:

By writing down while thinking, I can make the boundaries of my understanding precise which in turn
helps me focus on the minimal amount of information I am still lacking to make my thoughts
concrete.

Sometimes ideas sound good until you write them down.
