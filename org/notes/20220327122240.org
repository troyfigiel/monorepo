#+title: Circular probability distribution
#+filetags: :stub:

A circular distribution is a probability distribution for a random variable with angular values.
Any distribution on the real line can be converted to a circular distribution by wrapping it around
the unit circle. (See [[id:dcba589e-8195-4347-9be6-fb8ec12a9a7b][Wrapped distribution]])

Examples of circular distributions are:
- [[id:7c66b57f-e576-4823-a5c3-dacffa40ec26][Von Mises distribution]]
- [[id:2204790b-f4ae-4450-abfa-b12c7d720e5a][Circular uniform distribution]]
- [[id:dcba589e-8195-4347-9be6-fb8ec12a9a7b][Wrapped distribution]]
