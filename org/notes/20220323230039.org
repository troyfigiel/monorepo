#+title: Terminating Markov chain
#+filetags: :stub:

A terminating Markov chain is a Markov chain that solely has [[id:015d3737-7016-425e-be84-a65b510f69e7][Transient states]] with exception of a
single [[id:042db342-5cdf-4d7f-9220-1a5d052a45fc][Absorbing state]].

The [[id:f87cfbed-01da-4bcc-b89f-6a7510e0f213][Stochastic matrix]] of this process, may be written as

\begin{equation*}
P =
\begin{pmatrix}
T & \mathbf{T}^0 \\
\mathbf{0}^{\intercal} & 1
\end{pmatrix}
\end{equation*}

The time until absorption is modeled by a [[id:134015a8-f925-4604-bddb-45867ffec567][Discrete phase-type distribution]].
