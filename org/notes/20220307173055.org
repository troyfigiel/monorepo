#+title: CI/CD
#+filetags: :stub:

CI/CD stands for [[id:28df8744-03ab-4f03-8aa1-fb431d835930][Continuous integration]] and [[id:a864d414-5a34-4562-95b8-ea3330da9e42][Continuous delivery]] or [[id:d61ea814-0a7c-4145-9ffc-9da4c7879076][Continuous deployment]].

The faster the development cycle, the easier it is to stay on top of failures. This is why speed correlates with higher quality software.
