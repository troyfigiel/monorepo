#+title: ?Factorial moments of the binomial distribution
#+filetags: :appendix:stub:

With \( \mathbb{P}(X=k) = \binom{n}{k}p^k (1-p)^{n-k} \) we find

\begin{equation*}
\begin{align}
M_X(t)
&= \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} \binom{n}{k} (tp)^k (1-p)^{n-k} \\
&= (1 + (t-1)p)^n
\end{align}
\end{equation*}

Hence, \( \mathbb{E}[(X)_r] = (n)_r p^r \).
