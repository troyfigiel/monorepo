#+title: Sprint planning
#+filetags: :stub:

The sprint planning is a meeting where the [[id:7027bb9d-cacb-49b4-ba35-ccd38058e548][Product owner]], the [[id:f57fd375-53ba-445b-b505-55e7568889dd][Scrum master]] and the [[id:5ea5a00b-0cf2-44fd-a574-dc8cb4662d1e][Scrum team]] meet to discuss [[id:2b9aa9df-4a48-4611-b947-a3bb75d2ffaa][User stories]]
and estimate their relative sizes.

The top priorities are discussed and it is decided which [[id:2b9aa9df-4a48-4611-b947-a3bb75d2ffaa][User stories]] can go into the [[id:2b4cdf9e-e3fc-443c-9013-de7b5173ef98][Sprint backlog]].
