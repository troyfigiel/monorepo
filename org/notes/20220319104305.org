#+title: Every convergent Taylor series is an asymptotic series
#+filetags: :stub:

\[
\sum\limits_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(x_0)}{n!} (x-x_0)^n = f(x)
\]

And because \( x-x_0 \ll 1 \text{ as } x \rightarrow x_0 \) .
