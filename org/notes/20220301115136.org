#+title: McNemar's test
#+filetags: :stub:

McNemar's test is used for paired [[id:549fc149-c199-43e3-93c0-e5261bbf01e2][Bernoulli trial]] test whether paired nominal data come from the same Bernoulli distribution.

This is similar to the [[id:83cb1207-c9c3-4b04-938e-16f9bd0a9771][Paired t-test]], but for outcomes that follow a [[id:a52537ca-6373-4385-a4fd-0a0777e95dd7][Bernoulli distribution]].

Under the assumption that there are enough pairs with different outcomes, the test statistic
follows a [[id:107a2208-37c7-41b8-a4ff-dac2157c83ef][Chi-squared distribution]].

McNemar's test is the paired variant of the [[id:279bd970-5d55-425e-bef2-fe55539164b5][Binomial test]].
