#+title: Generalized harmonic number
#+filetags: :stub:

The generalized harmonic numbers are defined as

\[
H_{n, m} = \sum\limits_{k=1}^n \frac{1}{k^m}
\]

The special case \( m = 1 \) gives rise to the [[id:686b6d89-0f75-4c2d-aa65-fda8f0bd34b7][Harmonic numbers]].
