#+title: Linear regression does not mean the dependent variable depends linearly on the independent variable
#+filetags: :stub:

This can easily be a point of confusion: The linearity of a [[id:ded30554-7495-4218-9314-6145de6aa926][Linear regression]] model refers to
the dependence of the dependent variable on the parameters of the model.

As the most basic example, [[id:eb3bf387-4c70-4e6e-afdc-7d649765e0be][Polynomial regression]] is in fact a subset of [[id:ded30554-7495-4218-9314-6145de6aa926][Linear regression]].
