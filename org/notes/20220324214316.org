#+title: Second law of thermodynamics
#+filetags: :stub:

The [[id:6aa59df5-de7c-4fa9-8384-06ebe0cf771f][Entropy]] of an isolated system cannot decrease. They always arrive at a state of [[id:6e6b1431-ad32-4dcb-96d5-e20bce1748cc][Thermodynamic
equilibrium]] where the entropy is maximal for the given internal energy.
