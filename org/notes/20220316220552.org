#+title: Vega-Lite
#+filetags: :stub:

Vega-Lite is a simpler declarative specification aimed at statistical [[id:5aa3405a-5785-424a-ae15-f7d0ec5124ea][Visualization]]. The output of
Vega-Lite is [[id:a10826b3-7c1a-489b-9d99-8f05df288f22][Vega]]. It is an example of [[id:520ece5f-e0f1-45cb-bc4c-69557dc4afc2][Declarative programming]]. It is a [[id:77c09da7-ca77-428a-bed3-f6d81f424c7b][JSON]]-specification.
