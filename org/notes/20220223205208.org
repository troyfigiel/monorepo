#+title: Binomial theorem
#+filetags: :stub:

The binomial theorem states that we can expand

\[
(x + y)^n = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} \binom{n}{k} x^k y^{n-k}
\]

in terms of [[id:972139ed-384d-4f29-a609-60d87cf1d23a][Binomial coefficients]].
