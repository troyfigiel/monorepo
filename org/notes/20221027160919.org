#+title: Burndown chart
#+filetags: :stub:

A burndown chart shows the completed work against the projected rate of completion.
This chart will approach zero when work is being done.

The rate of progress is called [[id:7e07feec-b1cf-46e9-bf17-7612140ca4b9][Velocity]].
