#+title: Harmonic number
#+filetags: :stub:

The harmonic numbers are related to the [[id:0a8c9c6d-3089-4f1f-86c0-8c05e298172e][Harmonic mean]] due to the fact that

\[
H_n = \frac{n}{\mathrm{HM}(1, 2, ..., n)}
\]
