#+title: Kullback-Leibler divergence
#+filetags: :math:stub:

The KL divergence is not necessarily symmetric.

The fact that

\begin{equation*}
D_{\mathrm{KL}}(P || Q) \geq 0
\end{equation*}

follows from [[id:00336cf1-fbb7-4d61-ad3f-bc11a11c1d0a][Gibbs' inequality]].
