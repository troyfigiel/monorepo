#+title: Bidirectional causation
#+filetags: :stub:

For example, [[id:f590986d-8b66-4737-87d4-64f035b0f05b][Predator-prey equations]]. The lack of prey causes predator amounts to dwindle, but this
in turn causes prey amounts to rise. They have a bidirectional causal connection.
