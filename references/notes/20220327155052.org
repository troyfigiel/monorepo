#+title: Law of total cumulance
#+filetags: :stub:

The law of total cumulances allows us in particular to calculate the [[id:26df21fe-44fb-4463-95fd-c1fb183ef257][Cumulants]] of [[id:361223d4-c9ad-4595-b6e8-e071ee0eb987][Mixture models]]
and [[id:01cb19cb-7919-4dcb-8b20-8fd00027b442][Compound Poisson distributions]].

For special cases this leads to:
- [[id:f029d0a5-2391-4459-8d80-13d4b5c5e0db][Law of total expectation]]
- [[id:951ad4de-3f7a-4991-8fad-9519709dd555][Law of total probability]]
- [[id:37a9a6e0-897a-47e4-bfd3-c96d69a73839][Law of total variance]]
