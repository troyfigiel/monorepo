#+title: Lambda architecture
#+filetags: :stub:

The lambda architecture is a generic architecture that lets us handle [[id:8f45a23a-3f0b-4125-8701-fda7ba356b52][Data streams]].

Incoming data is sent to both a [[id:03942858-3492-4614-9223-3826dc36cdb5][Batch layer]] and a [[id:3b676062-8ab9-40ee-964e-d1da4b8a96c0][Speed layer]].

Predictions created from the [[id:3b676062-8ab9-40ee-964e-d1da4b8a96c0][Speed layer]] are then sent to the [[id:2e2634e1-3cc3-4e35-a6d1-4cbc404bb004][Serving layer]].

The name of this architecture comes from its topology, which resembles the Greek letter lambda.
