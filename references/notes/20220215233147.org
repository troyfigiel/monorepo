#+title: Sign test
#+filetags: :stub:

The sign test is a [[id:0215ff0c-a225-4488-8e56-d5050d101aea][Non-parametric test]] which is used for [[id:d6ab1e2f-f79e-42a4-a05c-b91c13a3c862][Paired data]] with a [[id:beba6f9c-e267-4314-90a5-71f150defc1f][Total order]].

For paired observations of random variables \( X \) and \( Y \), the sign test allows us to test the null
hypothesis \( X = Y \). [[id:5faa0d5c-7d24-4309-8262-b34d7338fb31][The sign test is a special case of the binomial test]].
