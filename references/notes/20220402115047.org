#+title: Gibbs measure
#+filetags: :stub:

The Gibbs measure is a [[id:623d4a27-b14a-487d-98e7-4245de36e4c6][Probability measure]]. It is a generalization of the [[id:96028615-0685-4eb2-9491-f065cec8d2a5][Canonical ensemble]].

For a specific microstate \( x \), the Gibbs measure is given by

\[
\mathbb{P}(X = x) = \frac{1}{Z(\beta)} e^{-\beta E(x)}
\]
