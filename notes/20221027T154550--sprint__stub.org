#+title: Sprint
#+filetags: :stub:

The sprint is a time span of one to three weeks in which the topics in the [[denote:20221027T155951][Sprint backlog]] are worked on.

During the sprint, [[denote:20221027T161847][Daily Scrum meetings]] are held for the [[denote:20221027T162906][Scrum team]].

The outcome of a sprint is a [[denote:20221027T154320][Potentially shippable product]].

After the sprint the [[denote:20221027T161857][Sprint review]] and [[denote:20221027T191157][Sprint retrospective]] are held.
