#+title: Confusion matrix
#+filetags: :stub:

The confusion matrix is a 2x2-dimensional table that visualizes the performance of a [[denote:20220310T194320][Classification]]
algorithm, most commonly a [[denote:20220311T132614][Binary classification]] algorithm. It is an example of a [[denote:20220313T170719][Contingency table]].
