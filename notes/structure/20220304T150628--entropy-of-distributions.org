* Definition
In the discrete case, the entropy of a random variable \( X \) is defined as

\[
H(X) = \mathbb{E}[- \log \mathbb{P}(X)]
\]

* Bernoulli distribution

\[
H(X) = - p \log p - (1-p) \log (1-p)
