#+title: Harmonic number
#+filetags: :stub:

The harmonic numbers are related to the [[denote:20220210T132205][Harmonic mean]] due to the fact that

\[
H_n = \frac{n}{\mathrm{HM}(1, 2, ..., n)}
\]

[[denote:20220320T131858][Euler-Mascheroni constant]]

[[denote:20220409T120819][The harmonic numbers are never integers]]
