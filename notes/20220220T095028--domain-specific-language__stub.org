#+title: Domain-specific language
#+filetags: :stub:

A domain-specific language (DSL) is a programming language specialized to a specific application domain.

Examples include:
- [[denote:20220220T094729][R]]

Languages in the [[denote:20220220T094628][Lisp]] family are also well-known to be able to easily create domain-specific
languages due to their [[denote:20220314T125800][Homoiconicity]].
