#+title: Kullback-Leibler divergence
#+filetags: :math:stub:

The KL divergence is not necessarily symmetric.

The fact that

\begin{equation*}
D_{\mathrm{KL}}(P || Q) \geq 0
\end{equation*}

follows from [[denote:20220326T224300][Gibbs' inequality]].
