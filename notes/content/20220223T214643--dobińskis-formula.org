* Relations
Dobiński's formula expresses the Bell numbers in terms of an infinite sum.

* Formal statement
The n-th Bell number \( B_{n} \) satisfies

\[
B_{n} = \frac{1}{e} \sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{k^n}{k!}
\]
