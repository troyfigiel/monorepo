#+title: The structure of my notes is modeled after the Zettelkasten principle
#+filetags: :stub:

The structure of my notes is reminiscent of the [[denote:20220221T233205][Zettelkasten]] principle. because is do believe that
[[denote:20220324T222310][Connecting ideas requires a different form of note taking from communicating ideas]].

However, I also believe that notes should develop organically and that the Zettelkasten terminology has a more restrictive meaning that I would like.
