#+title:      Degree of a differential form
#+date:       [2022-12-29 Thu 12:11]
#+filetags:
#+identifier: 20221229T121153

A form that can be integrated over a k-dimensional space, is called a k-form. The value of k is bounded between 0 and n, with 0-forms understood as differentiable functions. The n-forms (also called top-dimensional forms) are called volume forms [[denote:20221229T115021]].
