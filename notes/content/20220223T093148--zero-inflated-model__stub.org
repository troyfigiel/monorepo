#+title: Zero-inflated model
#+filetags: :stub:

A zero-inflated model is a statistical model based on a distribution that allows for frequent
zero-valued observations.

[[denote:20220304T133117][Zero-inflated models are mixture models]].

Zero-inflated models are similar to [[denote:20220304T133401][Hurdle models]] but not the same.
