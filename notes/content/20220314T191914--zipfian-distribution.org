#+title:      Zipfian distribution
#+date:       [2022-03-14 Mon 19:19]
#+filetags:
#+identifier: 20220314T191914

\[
f_{N, s}(k) = \frac{k^{-s}}{H_{N,s}}
\]

where \( H_{N,s} \) is the N-th [[denote:20220314T193141][Generalized harmonic number]].

Since \( \lim_{N \rightarrow \infty} H_{N, s} = \zeta(s) \), the limit of the Zipfian distribution
is the [[denote:20220314T192146][Zeta distribution]].
