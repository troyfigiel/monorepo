#+title: Lorenz system
#+filetags: :stub:

The Lorenz system is a system of differential equations of the form

\begin{equation*}
\begin{align}
\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t} &= \sigma (x-y) \\
\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t} &= x(\rho - z) - y \\
\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}t} &= xy - \beta z
\end{align}
\end{equation*}

The Lorenz system is particularly important in [[denote:20220306T193749][Chaos theory]]
