#+title: Second law of thermodynamics
#+filetags: :stub:

The [[denote:20220210T210007][Entropy]] of an isolated system cannot decrease. They always arrive at a state of [[denote:20220324T214503][Thermodynamic
equilibrium]] where the entropy is maximal for the given internal energy.
