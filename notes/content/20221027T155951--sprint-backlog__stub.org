The sprint backlog contains individual tasks from the [[denote:20221027T155544][Product backlog]] that need to be finished during a [[denote:20221027T154550][Sprint]].

It is important that the entire [[denote:20221027T162906][Scrum team]] and the [[denote:20221027T155224][Product owner]] have a clear understanding of what each [[denote:20221027T155728][User story]] involves.
