#+title: chi-squared distribution as sum of squares of normal distributions

\begin{theorem}
If \( X_i \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 1) \) are independent random variables, the random variable \( \sum\limits_{i = 1}^k X_i^2 \sim \chi^2(k).
\end{theorem}
