#+title: Gibbs measure
#+filetags: :stub:

The Gibbs measure is a [[denote:20220402T115115][Probability measure]]. It is a generalization of the [[denote:20220326T111110][Canonical ensemble]].

For a specific microstate \( x \), the Gibbs measure is given by

\[
\mathbb{P}(X = x) = \frac{1}{Z(\beta)} e^{-\beta E(x)}
\]
